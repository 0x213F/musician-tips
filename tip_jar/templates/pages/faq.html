Why is there a transaction fee?

What is the Musician Tips Dividend?

Everyone makes music, but not everyone is heard. The Musician Tips Dividend is split equally across all active musicians on the platform. Your contibution will help support your local San Francisco music scene.

It also funds the upkeep costs of this website (about $6/ month).

How do I start my own tip page?

For now, we only serve musicians performing in San Francisco. If you would like us to add support for your city, send us an email.

If you are a San Francisco based artist, give us a call so we can setup your tip page.

How much does it cost?

It is free.

How do you make money?

We do not make any money.

Where do I submit a feature request?

Before creating a feature request, browse pending feature requests. Once you have verified that your request is unique, create a new issue.

How can I contribute to musician.tips?

You may donate your labor by working on any open issues.

You can also financially sponsor the project to support site maintenance and encourage further development.

What is your data policy?

After a purchase, we securely store your email in our database. Your email address is never shared with anyone without your permission. The musician will have access to your email address if you agreed to share it with them.
