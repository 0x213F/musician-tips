What is the Musician Tips Dividend?

Everyone on this site makes music, but not everyone is heard. The Musician Tips Dividend is split equally across all active artists on the platform. Your contibution will help support the San Francisco music scene.

It also funds the continuous maintenance of this website (about $6/ month).

Where do I submit a feature request?

Before creating a feature request, browse pending feature requests. Once you have verified that your request is unique, create a new issue.

How can I contribute to musician.tips?

Yes! You may donate your labor by working on any accepted feature request.

You can also monetarily sponsor the project to support maintenance and encourage further development.

What is your data policy?

By default, we do not retain any of your information.

If you opt in to sharing your email, we securely store it in our database. It is never shared without your consent.
